Why property investment

POWERFUL AND STABLE

Property is a powerful investment yet stable - this type of asset has been increasing ever since but still the returns are still stable. It’s also an investment that acts as a hedge against inflation and the power to grow through leverage even if the economy is unstable.

LEVERAGE

Property is leverage - in simple terms, when it comes to property you can use other people’s money to increase the potential return of investment.

TAX BENEFITS

Tax benefits - The good thing about owning a property is that the government helps property owners with substantial tax benefits such as depreciation allowance given that the property is used to generate income, for example property held to generate rental income.

LEGACY

Legacy - The good thing about property is that it can be passed on from one generation to the other, Even if you are not alive, this type of investment can be beneficial for the future generation to come.

ENDURANCE

Property allows you operate in an imperfect market - unlike shares that are sold for the same price. In property you can use your knowledge and negotiation expertise to buy property considerable on below market price

Property Investment strategies

OPM (Other People’s Money) – In simplest terms when it comes to OPM strategy you buy a property with a loan and get tenants to pay it off for you.

HMO’s (Houses of Multiple Occupation) - Instead of letting your house or flat to one family tenant, you can let out other individual rooms to increase your income, cash flow & yield. The more rooms you have the higher the income you will get. Such as Bed & Breakfast, Student apartment.

Buy to Sell strategy - Usually here most investors often buy properties when they are being sold by distressed sellers. Buying, Refurbing and Selling residential property is also known as trading; those who happen to use this strategy are known as traders than investors.

Joint Venture Strategy – This one you buy properties in groups; you can either raise capital or buy properties through OPM strategy. The ownership is shared with a deed of trust protecting both parties interest.

Forex Trading Course

Each and every country has its own currency. When people want to travel they have to exchange Rands and buy the currency of the country they are visiting e.g. US Dollar. So that means that in order to exchange the local currency for the foreign currency they need to convert it at a specific rate. The currencies trade in pairs i.e USDZAR. So all Forex trading is, is buying and selling currencies. Since they trade in pairs, that means that when one currency rises the other currency is falling.

Why Forex Trading?

The foreign exchange market largest traded market in the world with over 5 trillion Dollars traded on a daily basis. Thus, it is the most liquid market in the world.

  • Accessibility - The market is open 24 hours a day 5 days a week. With access to the internet, it can be traded in the comfort of your own home thus giving you the ability to build wealth and supplement your income. You only need a few minutes per day in order to place your trades.

  • Transparency - With Forex what you see is what you get. As long as you are aware of expected news and read your charts, the are relatively no surprises you can be able to relatively predict market direction.

  • Affordable - You do not need a large amount of money to start trading due to the leverage that is offered by forex brokers.

  • Safe learning environment - Almost all brokers have Demo accounts which beginners can use to familiarize themselves with the trading platform as well as trading strategies without the risk of losing their own money.
