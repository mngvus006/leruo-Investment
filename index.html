About US

Founded in 2018 by a group of young professionals, Leruo Investment Club (LIC) is grounded on the principles of financial freedom and financial education of its members. The club has a clear mandate of educating its members about saving and investing in appreciating assets. The club is open to all South Africans who want financial growth. We plan to grow our money through a steady, long-term view, and responsible manner where we invest on JSE instruments that promise a gradual return with mitigated risks over time.

WHY CHOOSE US

Mutual Investment

At LIC we believe that together our money goes far. We apply the old traditional way of collection money through a Stokvel model, but with a different mandate. We do not save together for consumption however we save to invest our money into appreciating assets (shares, property).

Meeting of the Minds

LIC hosts monthly team meetings. The meetings are not only a platform to deliberate how as members of LIC we can grow our money as a stokvel, but also knowledge that can be used in our daily personal lives. We also encourage entrepreneurship within our members. We actively seek entrepreneurship/business opportunities for our members.

Business funding

LIC encourages its members to pitch their business idea and advertise products of their existing business at the meetings. The advertising service is free for LIC members, and can be made available to non-members at a small fee to showcase their products at LIC meetings.

Education

We promise to educate our members about financial principles. We envisage and pledge to go to different schools, stokvel clubs, and other community gatherings to share the knowledge of finances and the importance of saving and investing in appreciating assets.
